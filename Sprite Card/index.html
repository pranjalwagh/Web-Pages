Sprite

Sprite is an American-owned brand of clear, lemon and lime-flavored soft drink created by The Coca-Cola Company. Sprite comes in multiple flavors, including cranberry, cherry, grape, orange, tropical, ginger, and vanilla, and newer versions of the drinks are artificially sweetened.
